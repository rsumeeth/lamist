FAQ

Common Questions

What makes Lamnist different from other maternity hospitals?

Lamnist is a luxury boutique mother and child care hospital offering personalized attention, state-of-the-art facilities, and a compassionate team dedicated to making your pregnancy and delivery experience exceptional. Our combination of advanced technology, experienced specialists, and intimate care setting sets us apart.

What types of delivery options do you offer?

We offer all delivery options including natural vaginal delivery, water birth, cesarean section, and VBAC (Vaginal Birth After Cesarean). Our experienced obstetricians will discuss your birth plan and help you choose the safest option for you and your baby.

Do you have NICU facilities for newborn care?

Yes, we have a fully equipped Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) staffed by experienced neonatologists and pediatric nurses. Our NICU can handle premature births and other newborn complications, ensuring the best possible care for your baby.

Does Lamnist provide prenatal and postnatal support?

Yes, absolutely. We provide comprehensive prenatal care including regular check-ups, ultrasounds, and birth preparation classes. After delivery, we offer postnatal support including lactation counselling, baby care education, and emotional wellness support for new mothers.
